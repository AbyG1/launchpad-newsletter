What is Launchpad ?

One of the most prestigious events of IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn that is determined to serve the goal of creating opportunities for engineering students and graduates. Launchpad Kerala 2024 is a premier job fair that brings together talented individuals and innovative companies in the technical and engineering fields. This collaboration between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn aims to propel advancement and innovation within Kerala's employment landscape by facilitating the seamless exchange of ideas and expertise.

Call to Action

Event Roadmap

  1. Participant Registration
    Start your journey by registering for LaunchPad Kerala 2024. Complete the registration form to create your profile and express your interest in participating in the event.
  2. Join the community
    Join the Gtech Mulearn Discord Server and immerse yourself in our vibrant community. Engage with fellow participants, access resources, and participate in challenges to sharpen your skills.
  3. Technical Challenge
    Dive into our unique technical challenge designed to evaluate your technical and non-technical skills. Complete tasks, earn Karma points, and showcase your abilities to top companies.
  4. Online Aptitude Test
    Put your skills to the test in our online aptitude test. Demonstrate your capabilities and compete for opportunities to advance to the next stage of the selection process.
  5. Final Interview
    Shortlisted candidates will have the opportunity to participate in interviews conducted in Trivandrum, Kochi, or Kozhikode.Receive a confirmation email with your selected interview details. Gear up for the final stage of the selection process and showcase your potential to top recruiters.
